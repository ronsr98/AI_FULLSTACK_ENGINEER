Pan-Seared Steak

My favorite dish - a juicy steak with a perfect crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye steak, about 300g
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic + rosemary

Steps

  1. Take the steak out of the fridge 30 minutes before cooking.
  2. Dry it well and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the pan, add oil, and sear 2-3 minutes on each side.
  4. Lower the heat, add butter, garlic and rosemary.
  5. Rest the steak for 5 minutes, then slice and serve.
